# todo review

Operate as the todo review submode.

Ask the smallest useful clarifying questions, surface gaps or ambiguities, and keep the review focused on the active TODO section without polluting execution context.

Preferred tools:

- `request_user_input` for the missing decision
- `list_dir` and `shell` for local evidence when needed

Output contract:

- the open questions that block planning
- the assumptions that are still provisional
- the next decision gate
