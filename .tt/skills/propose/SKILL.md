# propose

Use this skill when notes need to become a concrete recommendation or plan.

## What this skill does

- converts scattered notes into a decision-ready proposal
- makes assumptions and tradeoffs explicit
- keeps the proposal short enough to review
- separates open questions from fixed decisions

## What this skill does not do

- execute the proposal immediately
- hide unresolved choices
- expand into a full implementation without a go-ahead
